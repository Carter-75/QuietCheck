## Design Guidelines

### Visual Style
- **Background**: Calming gradients (blues, purples, soft greens)
- **Typography**: Clean, modern sans-serif (Roboto, Inter)
- **Icons**: Minimalist, consistent style
- **Colors**: Match app theme (avoid harsh contrasts)
- **Spacing**: Generous whitespace for clarity

### Text Overlays
- **Position**: Top or bottom third of screen
- **Background**: Semi-transparent overlay for readability
- **Font Size**: Large enough to read in thumbnails
- **Color**: High contrast with background
- **Language**: Clear, benefit-focused, concise

### Content Guidelines
- **Real UI**: Use actual app screens (not mockups)
- **Realistic Data**: Show believable metrics and trends
- **No Personal Info**: Avoid real names, emails, or sensitive data
- **Consistent Branding**: Use app logo and colors throughout
- **Localization**: Prepare English version first, then localize

## Promo Video (Optional but Recommended)

### Specifications
- Duration: 30 seconds to 2 minutes
- Format: MP4 or MOV
- Resolution: 1920 x 1080 (Full HD)
- File size: Max 100MB

### Content Flow
1. **Hook (0-5s)**: "Feeling overwhelmed? QuietCheck can help."
2. **Problem (5-10s)**: Show stress indicators, burnout symptoms
3. **Solution (10-30s)**: Demonstrate app features
   - Mental load monitoring
   - Smart alerts
   - Recovery guidance
4. **Privacy (30-40s)**: "100% private, all data stays on your device"
5. **CTA (40-45s)**: "Download QuietCheck today. 7-day free trial."
